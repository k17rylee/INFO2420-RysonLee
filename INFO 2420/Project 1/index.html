Web Site Critiques

Trader Joes

Question #1:

What was your first impression of this site?

Answer:

My first impression of the site is how I felt like the website looked really appealing. The banner photo that they use has a lot of color and looks very pleasing. Outside of the very colorful banner photo, the website is very basic and easy to navigate.

Question #2:

How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)

Answer:

The site organizes the flow by having a main button right in the middle of the page that promotes the newest items and provides information on that item. They have different quick links along the left side to help take you where you want to go and what you want to find.

Question #3:

Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?

Answer:

The site structure makes sense to me. There isn't any area to get confused in.

Question #4:

Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?

Answer:

I believe the graphics, layout, and multimedia enhance the site. There is a very eye catching initial banner photo and everything else around it is very simple and not too fancy.

Question #5:

How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?

Answer:

Very quick. Little to no load time. It loaded instantly.

Question #6:

Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?

Answer:

it was readable and did not detract from the site. Font choice and size was good.

Question #7:

Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?

Answer:

There was a side bar on the left hand side as well as a pop down menu on the left top corner for links to more areas of the website.

Question #8:

Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?

Answer:

I wouldn't say there's a super obvious color scheme to the site. The background is white and there are a few buttons that are red with links to those pages so red and white would be the color scheme that they have if any.

Question #9:

How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?

Answer:

When I go to make the window smaller, the banner stays as the focal point of the window. It will shrink as you shrink the window to make sure that the whole banner is always shown in its entirety. It will drop the left quicklink bar as it gets smaller but the main banner is always visible.

Question #10:

What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Answer:

One thing that the website does well is the simplicity behind it and the ability to be colorful but not distracting.
